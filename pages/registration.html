Registration

Registrations will be done online through EasyConferences.org. Participants may pay instantly using their credit card (VISA/MASTERCARD), or by bank transfer. A one-stop-shop software is used for the whole registration process. The whole process is very easy to use, it is totally secure and can be completed in a few minutes.

  • Physical attendance: 150€ (early registration June 1st, 2025), 300€ (late registration)
  • Lunches and coffees (for 5 days): 50€
  • Social dinner: 25€
  • Student prices: please ask the organizers

How to register (in 5 simple steps)

  1. Complete Interest Form - Fill in the form with your details: [Fill Form]
  2. Create Account - Create an EasyConferences.org account: [Create Account]
  3. Verify email - Click on the activation link sent to your email
  4. Log In - After verifying your email, login to EasyConferences.org by clicking the folowing link: [Log In]
  5. Create Order - Create and settle your order online securely: [Order and Register]
